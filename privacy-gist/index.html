Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 28, 2026  ·  Effective immediately

Overview

Gist does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All processing happens entirely on your device.

Slate Automation operates the Gist mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains our approach to user data — which is, simply, that we do not collect any.

Data We Collect

Gist collects no personal data whatsoever. Specifically:

✓ No account required ✓ No text leaves your device ✓ No analytics ✓ No advertising IDs ✓ No crash reporting ✓ No third-party SDKs

Any text you enter into the app is processed locally and is never transmitted to Slate Automation's servers or any third party.

Third-Party Services

Gist does not integrate any third-party analytics platforms, advertising networks, or data brokers. The app makes no external network requests related to user data.

Children's Privacy

Gist does not knowingly collect data from any user, including children under the age of 13 (or the applicable minimum age in their jurisdiction). Since no data is collected from anyone, children are equally protected.

EU / EEA — GDPR

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (EU) 2016/679, data controllers must state a lawful basis for processing personal data. As Gist processes no personal data, GDPR obligations relating to data processing, storage, or transfer do not apply. No consent is required.

Vietnam — Decree 13/2023/NĐ-CP

In accordance with Vietnam's Decree on Personal Data Protection (Decree 13/2023/NĐ-CP, effective July 1, 2023), we confirm that Gist collects no personal data from users in Vietnam or any other jurisdiction. No data processing consent is required as no data is processed.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted at this URL with an updated "last updated" date. Your continued use of Gist after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
