Example workflow

AI support in action.

This n8n automation powers intelligent support routing. Messages come in, AI drafts responses, humans approve. Simple, reliable, yours to control.

Flow breakdown
  • 1Messages arrive from Intercom, Zendesk, or email and normalize into a single format.
  • 2AI classifies intent, priority, and sentiment, then drafts a reply in your voice.
  • 3Guardrails check PII, hallucination risk, and edge-cases before surfacing to agents.
  • 4Agents approve, edit, or escalate. Outcomes improve future responses.
