n8n example

A real support workflow — from message to resolution.

This n8n workflow powers AI-augmented support for a SaaS team. It routes messages, proposes responses, and keeps a human agent in the loop for final approval.

Flow overview
  • 1 New messages arrive from Intercom, Zendesk, or email and are normalized into a single format.
  • 2 An LLM classifies intent, priority, and sentiment, then drafts a reply in your brand voice.
  • 3 Guardrails check PII, hallucination risks, and edge-cases before surfacing to an agent inbox.
  • 4 Agents approve, edit, or escalate — outcomes are logged to improve prompts over time.
